My name is Billy Parvatam and I am a second year masters student at Virginia Tech studying communication. I am from Falls Church, Va, which is about 10 miles from Washington D.C. I studied my bachelor's at Virginia Tech as well, graduating with a B.A. in Multimedia Journalism with a minor in Political Science in 2019.

I am currently working as a Public Speaking Graduate Teaching Assistant at Virginia Tech. I teach 2 sections of Public Speaking comprising of about 80 students. My office is located in Shanks Hall 154.

My interests are in political communication. My thesis is a comparative study looking at how the media frames its coverage of Democratic Vice Presidential Nominee Kamala Harris and incumbent Vice President Mike Pence. I am specifically looking at newspaper coverage from The New York Times, The Sacramento Bee, and The Indianapolis Star. My chair is Dr. John Tedesco.

Future Goals

In the future, I would like to serve as a communications director or a digital director for an elected official. I have a passion for public service that I would like to put to use at the federal level I am excited to learn practical skills in this class that can help me down the road.
